The Jasper Daily: Trio Beach BBQ

Posted by: debbi July 18, 2011


We all headed out to Trio Beach with Martin and Anna for a beach BBQ. Jasper shoveled sand and tossed it into the ocean. He also tasted the sand and liked it so much that he went back for seconds! We then found a bbq spot and roasted some corn and had some yummy sausages. It was a really nice afternoon.
